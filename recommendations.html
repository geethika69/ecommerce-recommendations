How Our Recommendation System Works

Our hybrid recommendation system combines two approaches:

  • Collaborative Filtering: Suggests products based on what similar shoppers love (e.g., "Customers who bought this also bought").
  • Content-Based Filtering: Recommends items similar to those you've viewed (e.g., "More jackets like this").

This ensures personalized suggestions, even for new users.

Implementation Example

For a "Blue Denim Jacket," our system suggests:

  • Content-Based: Other denim jackets or casual wear.
  • Collaborative: Jeans or boots bought by similar users.
  • Hybrid: A mix, like a jacket and pants.
